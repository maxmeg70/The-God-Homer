How To Buy

Create a wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive $GDH tokens.

Send ETH to your wallet

You can transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure to use the ERC-20 network when transferring ETH.

Connect your wallet to Uniswap

Access your wallet to Uniswap by clicking "Connect Wallet" and selecting MetaMask.

Swap ETH for $GDH

You can start swapping as soon as you have ETH available! Press "Select a currency" and enter The GodHomer token address.
